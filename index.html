Scholarships
How to apply?

Scholarships will be awarded only to students, on the basis of merit. Applications to be considered for scholarships must be submitted before September 26, 2025. Results of the application process will be announced on Octuber 03, 2025.

The following information is required for the selection process:

  1. 1.- Personal Statement explaining why you should receive the scholarship (1 page, PDF)
  2. 2.- Abstract of your current research, that once accepted, will be presented in the school poster session (1 page, PDF)
  3. 3.- Curriculum Vitae (2 pages, PDF)
  4. 4.- A recommendation letter from your academic supervisor (PDF)

Students must complete the Application Form (below) and include all the requested information.

For the Parallel Workshop Sessions, students will be divided into Experimental or Modeling Activities based on the provided information.

Scholarship recipients are required to participate in the poster session included in the program. Relevant information regarding this session will be provided in due time.

At the poster session, participants should remain next to his/her poster to provide information about their contribution.

Participants are expected to attend the full set of academic activities.

About the Summer School

The main goal of the 3rd Asia-Pacific Combustion Institute Summer School is to introduce the combustion and fire safety community to fundamental combustion problems, focusing on studies of diffusion flames and their connection to large-scale fires. This year’s school will emphasize the link between combustion theory and practical applications in both industrial settings and real fire scenarios, contributing to create and strengthen the links between different academic institutions and research groups focused in the Asia Pacific and South American areas.
